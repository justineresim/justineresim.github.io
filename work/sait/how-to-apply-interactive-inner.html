How to Apply

01. Before You Apply

There are a few things you need to know before you begin your application. Make sure you're aware of your program's application and admission timelines. You will also need to know the term when your program starts - you'll be asked to select a term when you fill out the online application.

Keep track of your tasks

02. When You Apply

When you start your application, you'll be directed to ApplyAlberta - a centralized online application used by most Alberta post-secondary schools. You'll create an account through their Secure Identity and Access Management System (SIAMS) at the beginning of your application. Make sure you take note of your login information - you will need it if you apply to another program.

Keep track of your tasks

03. After you Apply

Within a week, you will receive an email acknowledging that SAIT has received your application. The email will include your SAIT ID number and instructions on how to login to mySAIT.ca and monitor your application status. Make sure you notify Student Services if your email address changes.
