The Career Exploration Centre is not just a place, it's an experience.

Our program helps youth learn about careers and themselves to see what fits, what sparks a passion and what fires their curiosity.

This program is suitable for both educators and community groups of up to 40 youth participants.

How it Works

Before you Come

Once your visit is confirmed, students will engage in online activities in the Resources for Students section of the site. This work takes less than an hour to complete and will start to build curiosity and familiarity around 30+ careers.

Morning

The day starts with a brief orientation and introduction to key concepts that will help participants think about careers. Later, visitors will have the opportunity to try up to three hands-on simulated career tasks, chosen from the 30+ career options they reviewed in advance. Activities include written self-reflection to identify and link interests to careers.


Lunch

For their 30-minute lunch break, participants will sit among SAIT students and absorb the rhythm of campus life.

Afternoon

For their 30-minute lunch break, participants will sit among SAIT students and absorb the rhythm of campus life.


After the Visit

After their visit, students can investigate and discover more about industries, careers and their interests by using the resources in the "Resources for Students" section of the website.
