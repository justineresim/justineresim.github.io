SAIT Summer Camps

Grades 4-12

SAIT Summer Camps is a summer camp program for youth entering grades 4-12, known for its hands-on learning environment and non-traditional offerings, SAIT Summer Camps encourages youth to explore interests and future career options, learn new things and make connections.

Career Exploration Centre

Grades 9-10

The Career Exploration Centre is a unique, experiential, learner-centered facility for youth in grades 9 and 10.

This is the place for students to get hands-on experience and make well informed career decisions based on real-world applications.
