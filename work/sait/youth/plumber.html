Plumber

Plumbers plan, install and service plumbing systems, fixtures, piping equipment and controls. These piping systems may be used to transport water, waste, gases or hot liquids.

Key Skills

  • Physical strength and stamina
  • Mechanical ability
  • Ability to work alone or with others

Working Conditions

  • Varied working conditions
  • May be exposed to outdoor weather conditions
  • Risk of injury

Is it a good fit?

You may be interested in this type of work if you like to:

  • Use tools to assemble things
  • Take things apart to see how they work
  • Fix broken things

When you are here

Hands on Activities

Visitors will get a chance to construct part of the drainage system in SAITCity's latest dream home by:

  • Installing the pipes, fittings and traps for the drainage system
  • Calculate the pipe slope to allow wastewater to drain properly
  • Install a vent to protect the home from poisonous gas
