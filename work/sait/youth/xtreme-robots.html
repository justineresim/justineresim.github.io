About the Camp

Come join our Minecraft™ company and manage your first project from start to finish! Learn how to work as a team, manage resources, work to meet deadlines and maintain your accounts as you compete against other teams. On the final day, present your findings and recommendations in a form of a business report, and find out if you truly learned the economics of Minecraft™.
