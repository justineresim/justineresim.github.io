We are Different from most Day Camps

75 Uniquely Focused Camps

Our fun and focused environment supports individual growth, independence and self-awareness. Each year, the summer camps community consists of aspiring culinary artists, scientists, business leaders, athletes, engineers and tradespeople, among those that are interested in using the program to sharpen skills and discover new ones.

Most camps are one week in length and are linked to a SAIT program. Many camps are taught by SAIT instructors or industry professionals.
