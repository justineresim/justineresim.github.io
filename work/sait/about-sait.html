The Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT) is built on passion - shared by our students, faculty and staff - to shape new worlds as entrepreneurs, leaders and community builders.

Action Based Learning

A SAIT education is designed to provide students with practical experience as well as theoretical knowledge. Our classrooms and unique labs provide space for hands-on learning while many programs offer practicums and integrate projects based in real-world industry needs.
