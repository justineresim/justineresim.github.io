Applied Research and Innovation Services

Applied Research and Innovation Services (ARIS) at SAIT Polytechnic unites the needs of industry with the knowledge and skills of faculty and students to bring innovations closer to market.

For Students For Industry

How We Work

ARIS delivers operations and support services to industry and internal partners at all stages of collaboration. Service starts with the initial inquiry about engaging SAIT in an applied-research project.

ARIS involves students in real-world industry research projects through practicum placement, capstone projects and as student research assistants.
