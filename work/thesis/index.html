about.


Wired Rooms is a must have for anyone redecorating. Easy to use, Wired Rooms makes it simple to test out new decor combinations and plan any beautiful space.

Available on any device, Wired Rooms is a convenient tool that allows you to create, preview, save, and coordinate even “on-the-go”.


BE INSPIRED
Browse through hundreds of living room items and inspirational photos.

DESIGN
Create an ideaboard for your next home decorating project. Add items from the collection as well as images of your own.

SHARE
Save your projects to your device, email to friends, post to facebook and flicker.

tech.

HTML5: Main coding used to layout the site.

CSS3: Wired Rooms artistic design is created with CSS3.

JavaScript: User interactivity is created with JavaScript. The main functionality of the site is contained to one page, producing a very easy, efficient tool for users.

PHP: The site contains a database for which PHP is used to access. The database contains a library of items for users to search/browse and also allows users the ability to save their projects.

developer.

Justine Lileikis, designer and developer of Wired Rooms, has always been interested in visual communication. She has a degree from The University of Western Ontario, Honors Specialization in Visual Arts. After completing her degree she decided to widen her skill set by entering Fanshawe's Interactive Media Design Specialist Program. Coming from Western as a Visual Art Student with no coding background the leap to Interactive Media was a challenge, but one she took on with enthusiasm and determination.

Justine’s unique background gives her an edge over the competition by creating interactive media projects that are both visually appealing and can function with the user experience in mind.
